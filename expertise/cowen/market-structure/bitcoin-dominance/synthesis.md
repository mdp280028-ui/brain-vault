# Bitcoin Dominance

BTC's market-cap share of the total crypto market. Cowen tracks dominance as the primary lens for altcoin-season timing — falling dominance signals capital rotation into alts, rising dominance signals BTC-favoring regime.

## Current Thesis

BTC dominance excluding stablecoins continues making higher highs, with headline dominance suppressed only by rising stablecoin dominance — capital sheltering in stables, not rotating into alts. The macro backdrop reinforces the BTC-favoring regime: zero cuts priced through 2027 with a hike more probable than a cut, removing the liquidity catalyst altcoins require, and the risk-curve hierarchy (alts annihilated vs BTC, BTC hit harder vs SPX) structurally confirms capital does not rotate down the risk curve during drawdowns. The new brief shifts the longer-term BTC frame from bearish to constructive — the supply-in-profit/loss crossover has occurred, which Cowen treats as the historical permission condition for a cycle bottom forming within 1–4 months — but this does not signal altcoin rotation: the accumulation thesis is BTC-specific, framed as a DCA opportunity into BTC ahead of a 2027–2028 bull phase, leaving the BTC-dominance-over-alts dynamic intact through the bottoming window.

## Key Evidence

- BTC dominance excluding stablecoins still making higher highs — headline suppression is stablecoin dominance rising, not alts outperforming (mIjviu7Q4ag)
- The A-B-C-D pattern on prior BTC dominance charts drove dominance "far longer than most people thought" — Cowen treats this as the direct precedent for continued BTC-favoring regime (mIjviu7Q4ag)
- Stablecoin dominance (USDT + USDC combined) weekly close below the 21-week EMA then bounce is an exact replay of the 2022 pattern where false breakdowns each preceded resumed bear market conditions (7byCfkDQxq8, mIjviu7Q4ag)
- Total 2 (minus USDT) / BTC ratio in relentless decline as rate-cut hopes evaporate; zero cuts priced through 2027 with a hike more likely than a cut removes the macro catalyst alts require (Rf-gAQJk5-0)
- BTC supply-in-profit/loss lines have now crossed — historically the cycle bottom has never preceded this crossover, meaning all prior altcoin-rotation calls were premature; the crossover now permits a BTC cycle low within 1–4 months but does not open the altcoin trade (vxnpP3EOl-8)
- The risk-curve hierarchy — alts annihilated vs BTC in prior cycles, now BTC hit harder vs SPX — confirms capital does not rotate into lower-risk-curve assets during drawdowns, reinforcing BTC dominance over alts until a full bull phase resumes (OuHBTQ6fkfQ)
