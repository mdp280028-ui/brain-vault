# Bull Market Support Band

A combined indicator (20-week SMA + 21-week EMA) Cowen treats as the bull/bear regime line for Bitcoin. Sustained closes below it signal bear-market continuation; reclaims signal regime shifts. Also applied to SPX and select altcoins.

## Current Thesis

The 20-week SMA / 21-week EMA band has moved from acting as bear-market resistance (rejecting BTC on the way up) to being broken to the downside — Bitcoin has now fallen back below the band on the daily chart, with the weekly 20-week SMA sitting at ~$74K and BTC trading roughly $1,000 beneath it. The recent rally had minimal follow-through before retesting the band, a pattern Cowen explicitly contrasts with 2023's more sustained bounce, and one he reads as the classic midterm fake-out: bulls briefly celebrate a band reclaim, then price collapses back below. The band's breakdown confirms the standard midterm bear sequence Cowen has documented in 2018 and 2022 — rally to 21-week EMA, push to 200-day MA, retrace, one more bounce, then roll lower — and leaves the BTC/SPX ratio rejection at the same level and stablecoin dominance's false 21-week EMA breakdown as additional confirming signals. Cowen will not turn constructive on any rally until prices move materially higher; his regime change signal remains a sustained reclaim of the band, not a brief touch.

## Key Evidence

- Bitcoin has fallen back below the bear market resistance band on the daily chart; the weekly 20-week SMA sits at ~$74K with BTC ~$1,000 below it, awaiting a weekly close to confirm the weekly breakdown (slTIiS-Y65k)
- The recent rally produced minimal follow-through before retesting the band — far less than 2023 — matching the classic midterm fake-out pattern where BTC briefly pokes above, bulls declare victory, then price collapses (slTIiS-Y65k)
- Bitcoin rejected at the 20-week SMA / 21-week EMA band in the current midterm year, matching the pattern from 2014, 2018, 2019, and 2022 midterm bear cycles (dy3E7Jzte88)
- The BTC/SPX ratio was also rejected at the same 20-week SMA / 21-week EMA level, extending the bear-regime signal beyond spot BTC to relative performance (dy3E7Jzte88)
- Stablecoin dominance (USDT + USDC) had a weekly close below the 21-week EMA before bouncing back — an exact replay of the 2022 false breakdown that preceded bear-market resumption (7byCfkDQxq8)
- In every prior cycle (2014, 2018, 2022), Bitcoin rallied to or above the 200-day MA during the bear market and bulls declared the cycle over; the band's bear-regime read prevailed each time, and Cowen sees no basis for treating the current setup differently (7byCfkDQxq8)
