# BTC Cycle Theory

Cowen's framework that Bitcoin and broader risk assets follow an approximately four-year cycle of bull-top, bear-bottom, and accumulation phases. He frames the cycle as macro-driven rather than halving-driven, citing parallel equity-market cycle precedents going back to the 1950s.

## Current Thesis

BTC is in Stage 3 of the bear market (June–October 2026), with the cycle bottom pinned at approximately day 1,424–1,432 from the cycle low — roughly late Q3/early Q4 2026. The first weekly close below the 200-week MA this cycle (June 2026) mirrors the same event in June 2018 and June 2022, and the 2026 price structure (Feb low → higher low late March/early April → lower low June) is a near-exact repeat of 2018 — reinforcing rather than disrupting the cycle roadmap. Cowen's base-case sequence remains: early-summer low → mid-to-late summer countertrend rally → final cycle bottom late Q3/early Q4, with the default assumption being time-based capitulation; however, he explicitly holds open a pivot if price-based capitulation (massive volume spike, full on-chain reset) occurs first. The four-year cycle rule — buy at end of midterm years, sell in Q4 of the post-halving year — remains undefeated across three cycles, and multiple on-chain conditions (MVRV Z-Score not yet below zero, BTC not yet below realized price ~$53K, no volume capitulation spike) confirm the low is not yet in.

## Key Evidence

- BTC topped on cycle day 1,162 — within one week of the prior two cycles' tops; the ROI-from-low indicator places the bottom at approximately day 1,424–1,432, pointing to late Q3/early Q4 2026. (7byCfkDQxq8, QMn7cm4nfYU, psz9FwqWGns, g4FiHz14W14)
- The 2026 price structure (low Feb → higher low late March/early April → lower low June) is a near-exact repeat of 2018; BTC closing below the 200-week MA in June 2026 mirrors June 2018 and June 2022, with the 2018 analog pointing to a brief July bounce then a sell-off to the cycle-equivalent of ~$60K before the final bottom. (1LI9wfqxOmc)
- Every prior bear market bottom (2014, 2018, 2022) featured a massive volume capitulation spike that reset all on-chain metrics; that spike has not occurred yet, which is Cowen's primary reason for rejecting any current bottom call — "we just simply haven't had anything like that this bear market yet." (psz9FwqWGns, g4FiHz14W14, 1LI9wfqxOmc)
- Every midterm-year bear market has lasted approximately 50–60 weeks; prior cycles bottomed at days 364–406 from the peak, while the current cycle remains well short of those thresholds. (psz9FwqWGns, hXlR5xu0HK4, g4FiHz14W14)
- The MVRV Z-Score (currently 0.251, approaching zero) and realized price (~$53K, not yet breached) are both unsatisfied bottom conditions; every prior cycle saw MVRV cross below zero and BTC dip below the realized price before the bull market began. (hXlR5xu0HK4, g4FiHz14W14, R9vEpIpyj2s)
- Cowen's distilled rule — buy BTC at the end of midterm years, sell at the end of post-halving years — is confirmed undefeated across three cycles; recommended strategy is DCA in the second half of midterm years rather than trading countertrend rallies. (slTIiS-Y65k, Tj7mvTHDe1U, psz9FwqWGns, vTcABO_0Exg, hXlR5xu0HK4, 5nIhiijBQt4, g4FiHz14W14, OYcGIifaO4Q, 1LI9wfqxOmc)
