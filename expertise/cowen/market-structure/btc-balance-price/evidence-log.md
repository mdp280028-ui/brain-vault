# Evidence log: Btc Balance Price

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-06-26 (WrYTIBG10vI): Cowen defines the balance price (~$38K, within the $30K–$40K range) as the single non-time-based indicator that would confirm full price-based Bitcoin capitulation. He notes Bitcoin has historically spent only a few days below this level — observable in 2011, 2015, 2018, and 2022 — making any touch extremely significant. The key mechanism: "If it does happen that would be price based capitulation. To go to that level would make it a lot easier for a lot of people to say the bare market is over. Because then all the onchain indicators will be fully reset." [00:37] He is explicit that this is conditional, not a directional prediction.
