# Evidence log: June Seasonal Turning Point

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-14 (zKHgKnv51r0): Cowen presents a four-cycle pattern showing that June consistently acts as a pivot for Bitcoin in bear markets: rallying into June (2014, 2019) produced June highs; falling into June (2018, 2022) produced June lows. Because Bitcoin is still rising in mid-May 2026, he concludes a June top is the higher-probability outcome, with the 0.382 Fibonacci level (~$85K) converging with trendline resistance as the specific target by that time. The 2019 analog reinforces the timing: "it it would seem to me like the most likely outcome is that it will likely go down into Q4." [15:01]

He targets mid-to-late June specifically and flags a potential BoJ rate hike as the event-driven catalyst, noting BTC bottomed roughly one week after the July 2024 hike. "The window of weakness will likely persist for a few more weeks, probably into June, mid to late June seems about right." [14:57]
