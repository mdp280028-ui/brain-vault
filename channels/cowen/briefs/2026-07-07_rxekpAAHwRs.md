## Key points (the distilled video)
1. Cowen draws a direct structural analogy between Bitcoin's price action in 2018 and in 2026.
2. In 2018: low in February, higher low in late March/early April, lower high in May, then the June low swept the February low, landing at 5,700.
3. In 2026: the same sequence occurred — low in February, higher low in late March/early April, lower high in May (at the 200-day moving average), and a June sweep of the February low.
4. The 2026 June sweep low landed at 57,000 — exactly 10x the 2018 equivalent level (5,700).
5. The May 2026 lower high specifically formed at the 200-day moving average, a technical detail not present in his description of the 2018 analog.
