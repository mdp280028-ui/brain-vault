# Evidence log: Btc On Chain Reset Pivot Conditions

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-06-14 (psz9FwqWGns): Cowen explicitly defines the conditions under which he would pivot away from his October 2026 base case: a price-based capitulation — a drop of roughly $20,000 from ~$63K — that resets all on-chain indicators (realized price, MVRV-Z, volume spike, supply in profit/loss crossover). He uses March 2020 as the template, noting that crash justified an early pivot precisely because it produced a complete on-chain reset. Until that occurs, he states: [05:23] "If Bitcoin were to capitulate in June, the same way that it did in March of 2020, then it wouldn't make sense in my opinion to stay bearish until October. It just wouldn't." He also notes that even a drop to ~$54K (realized price) would likely not fully reset the MVRV-Z score, meaning partial resets do not satisfy his pivot condition.

- 2026-06-25 (g4FiHz14W14): Cowen sharpened his price-based capitulation threshold to the balance price (~$38K), describing it as the point where "who cares when it happens" — timing becomes irrelevant because on-chain risk metrics would be fully reset. He contrasted this with a mere dip below the realized price (~$53K), which he expects but does not treat as sufficient for a full reset, noting the MVRV-Z at 0.251 must still cross below zero and the on-chain risk composite must fall from ~0.2 toward the ~0.1 level seen at prior cycle lows. A continued absence of a volume capitulation spike — matching the pattern from 2018 and 2022 bottoms — further confirms the reset condition has not been met. [10:39] "I would say if we go to the balance price. Bitcoin historically doesn't spend very much time below the balance price. Just a few days."

- 2026-06-26 (WrYTIBG10vI): In a short definitional clip, Cowen identified a touch of the balance price (~$38K, within the $30K–$40K range) as the single clearest signal of full price-based capitulation, noting that Bitcoin has historically spent only a few days below this level across 2011, 2015, 2018, and 2022. He frames it conditionally rather than as a prediction: "If it does happen that would be price based capitulation… because then all the onchain indicators will be fully reset." [00:37/00:47] The significance is that reaching that level removes all ambiguity — it would simultaneously satisfy every on-chain reset condition, making the bear market bottom call straightforward for market participants.
