# TOTAL3 vs BTC Dominance

TOTAL3 (total altcoin market cap excluding BTC and ETH) plotted against BTC dominance. Cowen uses this overlay to size altcoin-season magnitude and identify altcoin-cycle peaks distinct from the BTC cycle.

## Current Thesis

No direct TOTAL3 or BTC dominance chart has appeared in reviewed briefs, but Cowen now offers a structural model-based explanation for why altcoin seasons fail to materialize: durable rotation into altcoins requires Bitcoin to reach the 95th percentile or higher on his asymmetric quantile model, a threshold Bitcoin missed in both 2019 and 2025 (topping near the 75th percentile both times). Because BTC has not achieved that euphoric upper-tail price action, the precondition for TOTAL3 expansion and BTC dominance decline has not been met, and Cowen sees no mechanism that changes this until a future cycle produces a deeper euphoria phase. The midterm year weakness thesis implies BTC is more likely to test sub-Q1 downside in late 2026 than to generate the upper-tail breakout that would ignite altcoin rotation.

## Key Evidence

- In both 2019 and 2025, BTC topped near the 75th percentile on the asymmetric quantile model — well short of the 95th–99th percentile range that historically coincides with full euphoria and sustained altcoin rotation (uFn3KUE-VTI).
- Cowen explicitly links the absence of a durable alt season in 2019 and 2025 to BTC failing to become euphoric: "Bitcoin never became euphoric because it topped out before getting to the 95th percentile." [25:20] (uFn3KUE-VTI).
- Both 2019 and 2025 cycle tops occurred roughly two months before QT ended and the Fed balance sheet began expanding, suggesting monetary policy timing also capped BTC's upper tail (uFn3KUE-VTI).
- Cowen defers BTC's own cycle recovery to the next market cycle, pushing out the precondition for altcoin rotation and TOTAL3 expansion (PnveYgxtd1w).
- BTC is currently at the 9.4th historical percentile with midterm downside scenarios to $48–58K, making an upper-tail euphoria breakout — the trigger for altcoin season — structurally unlikely near-term (uFn3KUE-VTI).
