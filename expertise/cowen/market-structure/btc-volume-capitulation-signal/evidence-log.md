# Evidence log: Btc Volume Capitulation Signal

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-06-14 (psz9FwqWGns): Cowen identifies the volume capitulation spike as a necessary marker of every historical Bitcoin bear market bottom, citing 2014, 2018, and the March 2020 crash as examples where a "wall" of weekly volume clearly marked the low. He argues the current cycle has produced no such spike, which is a primary reason he does not believe the bottom is in. His direct quote: [11:27] "literally every example of every bear market that Bitcoin has ever had, it hasn't actually ended until you get a big spike in volume." He expects this volume event to arrive alongside the Q4 2026 final low, potentially coinciding with a stock market decline.
