# Bull Market Support Band

A combined indicator (20-week SMA + 21-week EMA) Cowen treats as the bull/bear regime line for Bitcoin. Sustained closes below it signal bear-market continuation; reclaims signal regime shifts. Also applied to SPX and select altcoins.

## Current Thesis

The 20-week SMA / 21-week EMA band is currently acting as bear market resistance rather than support — Bitcoin has been rejected at this level, consistent with behavior in all prior midterm bear years (2014, 2018, 2019, 2022). The BTC/SPX ratio has also been rejected at the same band, confirming cross-asset bearish regime. Cowen treats the rejection as confirmation that the bear market continues, with a sweep of the April 2025 low (~$60K) called highly probable within the next couple of months.

- Bitcoin rejected at the 20-week SMA / 21-week EMA band in the current midterm year, matching the pattern from 2014, 2018, 2019, and 2022 midterm bear cycles (dy3E7Jzte88)

- Two consecutive red weekly closes in BTC materially raise the probability of a June low, consistent with the thesis that rejection at the band signals continuation (dy3E7Jzte88)
- The 20-week cycle between successive bear-market lows (observed in 2014 and 2018) places the next BTC low in late June — week 16 already elapsed as of the video date (dy3E7Jzte88)
- Rate-hike pricing and elevated energy prices reinforce the bear-regime read the band is already signaling (dy3E7Jzte88)
