## Current Thesis

Cowen uses crowd sentiment polls as a contrarian indicator, but with an important nuance: the majority is not automatically wrong. A 60/40 bearish split means the majority is simply correct; the contrarian signal only fires when consensus reaches 75–80% on one side. As of June 2026, roughly 60–65% of respondents are bearish on Bitcoin, which Cowen reads as the crowd correctly identifying the bear market — not yet an extreme that signals a reversal. The bear market ends when that consensus reaches near-unanimity, which he expects to coincide with the Stage 3 completion around October 2026.
