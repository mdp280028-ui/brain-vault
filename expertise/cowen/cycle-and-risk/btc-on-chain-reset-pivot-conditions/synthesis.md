## Current Thesis

Cowen's default framework is time-based capitulation — following the cycle's historical duration to an expected Q4 2026 low — but price-based capitulation can override it if a sharp drop fully resets all on-chain indicators. The clearest signal for that reset would be a touch of the balance price (~$38K), where Bitcoin has historically spent only a few days; at that level, calling the bottom becomes unambiguous regardless of timing. Short of that, the on-chain composite (currently just below 0.2, vs. historical lows near 0.1) and MVRV-Z (currently 0.251, not yet below zero) both indicate the reset has not yet occurred. A drop below the realized price (~$53K) is expected but insufficient on its own to constitute full capitulation.
