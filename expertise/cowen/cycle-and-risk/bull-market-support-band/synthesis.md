# Bull Market Support Band

A combined indicator (20-week SMA + 21-week EMA) Cowen treats as the bull/bear regime line for Bitcoin. Sustained closes below it signal bear-market continuation; reclaims signal regime shifts. Also applied to SPX and select altcoins.

## Current Thesis

The 20-week SMA / 21-week EMA band continues to act as bear-market resistance — Bitcoin remains below it, and Cowen's structural bear view is unchanged. The new nuance from this brief is that the band now serves as a counter-trend rally target: if Bitcoin can hold the 200-week EMA for a second consecutive weekly close, Cowen sees meaningfully higher odds of a bounce back toward the band in late July or early August, but frames this explicitly as a low-conviction counter-trend move within the ongoing bear, not a regime change. Prior rejections at the band (May 2026, matching 2018 and 2022 patterns) remain the defining evidence; the band's role as the regime-change signal is intact — a sustained reclaim would be constructive, but Cowen's base case is that any rally back to it will be another rejection, with the final bear low still expected in Q4.

## Key Evidence

- Bitcoin rejected at the 20-week SMA / 21-week EMA band in the current midterm year, matching the pattern from 2014, 2018, 2019, and 2022 midterm bear cycles (dy3E7Jzte88)
- The BTC/SPX ratio was also rejected at the same 20-week SMA / 21-week EMA level, extending the bear-regime signal beyond spot BTC to relative performance (dy3E7Jzte88)
- The recent rally produced minimal follow-through before retesting the band — far less than 2023 — matching the classic midterm fake-out pattern where BTC briefly pokes above, bulls declare victory, then price collapses (slTIiS-Y65k)
- Stablecoin dominance (USDT + USDC) had a weekly close below the 21-week EMA before bouncing back — an exact replay of the 2022 false breakdown that preceded bear-market resumption (7byCfkDQxq8)
- In the 2018 analog, Bitcoin rallied in May slightly above the bear market resistance band before rolling into a June low — Cowen sees the band as the likely ceiling on any counter-trend rally into July/August 2026 (SG3tuA8zqs8)
- A counter-trend rally back toward the band is now conditionally possible if the 200-week EMA holds, but Cowen treats it as a low-conviction move within the bear, not a regime shift (SG3tuA8zqs8)
