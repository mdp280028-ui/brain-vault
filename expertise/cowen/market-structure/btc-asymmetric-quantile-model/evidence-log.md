Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-30 (uFn3KUE-VTI): Cowen introduces a rigorously fitted asymmetric quantile model that applies distinct curvature to Bitcoin's lower and upper price tails, explicitly crediting Giovani (power law) and Real Plan C (exponential decay upper tail) as predecessors.
