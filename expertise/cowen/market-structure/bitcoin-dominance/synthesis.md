# Bitcoin Dominance

BTC's market-cap share of the total crypto market. Cowen tracks dominance as the primary lens for altcoin-season timing — falling dominance signals capital rotation into alts, rising dominance signals BTC-favoring regime.

## Current Thesis

BTC dominance excluding stablecoins continues making higher highs, with the BTC-over-alts regime now reinforced by three converging forces: declining social risk keeping retail absent, a hawkish macro backdrop removing any liquidity catalyst, and the 2018/2019 historical analogs pointing to altcoin underperformance while BTC moves sideways or lower. The new risk from this brief is the 2018 analog specifically: in July 2018, the altcoin market was "annihilated" while Bitcoin traded sideways, and Cowen sees declining social interest and fewer retail participants making a repeat of that dynamic plausible for July 2026. With BTC itself trapped below the bear market resistance band and likely to eventually break lower, no conditions exist for capital rotation into alts — the BTC-favoring regime remains structurally intact through the bottoming window.

## Key Evidence

- BTC dominance excluding stablecoins still making higher highs; as social interest trends down, dominance trends up — a relationship Cowen says has held since 2021 (gAf6tPzkcgg)
- Social risk composite (Google Trends, Coinbase app ranking, YouTube subscribers/views, Wikipedia page views, Twitter followers) in sustained decline; crypto YouTube channels now losing ~400–500 subscribers/week vs. gaining 40,000–60,000/week at the 2021 peak (gAf6tPzkcgg)
- 2018 analog adds a new altcoin-specific risk: "the altcoin market in 2018 bled out while Bitcoin was going sideways... the altcoin market got annihilated in July 2018, and Bitcoin was basically doing okay" — declining social interest makes this repeat plausible in July 2026 (d71wYhwKpFY)
- 2019 analog quantitatively confirmed: Fed cut exactly three times for 75 bps in both 2019 and 2025; QT ended ~two months after BTC price peak in both cycles — "apathetic top" with no retail return and no altcoin rotation (gAf6tPzkcgg)
- Total 2 (minus USDT) / BTC ratio in relentless decline as rate-cut expectations fully evaporate; zero cuts priced through 2027, with hikes more likely than cuts, removing the macro catalyst alts require (Rf-gAQJk5-0)
- Coinbase tokenized stocks rollout structurally crowds out altcoin demand by consolidating BTC, alts, and equities on one platform — a structural headwind for altcoin dominance recovery (xutthOFM_RA)
