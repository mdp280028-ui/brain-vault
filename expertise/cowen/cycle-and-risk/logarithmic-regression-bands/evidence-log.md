# Evidence log: Logarithmic Regression Bands

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-21 (mwrgOz852wI): Cowen's ETH logarithmic regression band model defines three levels — lower support, fair-value midline, and bear market resistance — and all three are active in this video. ETH has been rejected at the resistance band and he expects a move down to the lower band, which coincides exactly with the April 2025 lows. He cites the model's long-run accuracy as context: "I was like, gosh, it's going to take until, you know, 2026 for just the fair value of Ethereum according to the regression model to go above $2,000, right?" [01:47]. A durable close below the lower band would signal recession (as in March 2020); reaching it without a recession would trigger his expectation of a sizable counter-trend rally.
