# Bitcoin Risk Metric

Cowen's proprietary 0-to-1 scaled composite indicator used to identify zones of long-term accumulation (low risk) and distribution (high risk). Buy zones cluster below 0.3 and distribution zones above 0.7; the metric is central to his long-horizon allocation framework.

## Current Thesis

As of mid-June 2026 the summary risk metric reads 0.296 — still within the buy zone Cowen has defined for this cycle (below 0.3), risen from sub-0.2 readings earlier in the month. The metric anchors a tiered dynamic DCA framework ($100/week at 0.2–0.3, $200 at 0.1–0.2, $300 at 0–0.1), with no buying above 0.3 and no selling until 0.6. The June 21 brief adds macro context without updating the metric reading: Bitcoin's continued weakness is consistent with its position at the far end of the risk curve in a high-rate, QT environment, reinforcing why the accumulation zone has persisted — the regime that would reset the metric toward distribution requires a Fed pivot, which Cowen sees as conditional on equity weakness in late Q3/early Q4 2026. October 2026 remains the modal expectation for the cycle low.

## Key Evidence

- Current summary risk reading of 0.296 sits just below the 0.3 buy ceiling, confirming continued accumulation conditions as the second half of the midterm year approaches (hx_neha7BVQ).
- Bitcoin has historically spent only 14.73% of its days in the 0.2–0.3 band, 12.5% in 0.1–0.2, and 2.34% (135 days total) below 0.1 — scarcity that underpins the dynamic DCA premium (hx_neha7BVQ).
- A DCA simulation shows equal-weight $30/week since 2014 ($18.3K invested) yields ~$700K; the same capital deployed dynamically below 0.3 yields ~$2.1M — a ~3x improvement (hx_neha7BVQ).
- Social risk is now lower than at the June 2022 low even though price risk is at the same level, indicating this accumulation zone is accompanied by historically deep apathy (hx_neha7BVQ).
- Prior sub-0.2 readings (as low as ~0.18–0.198) placed Bitcoin in the third and final bear market stage; sub-0.2 readings cluster most frequently in midterm years of the 4-year cycle (Di8YR9nX8Q8).
- Macro regime explains the metric's persistence in accumulation territory: crypto is "the furthest thing up the risk curve," and prolonged high rates / QT keep the frothiest assets suppressed until a Fed pivot materializes (5feBKA1-w-Q).
