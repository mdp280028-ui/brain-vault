## Key points (the distilled video)
1. Cowen dislikes sitting through Bitcoin's typical 70–80% bear-market drawdowns and has reduced exposure accordingly — but still holds some Bitcoin.
2. He believes Bitcoin is putting in its June low right now and that it makes sense to start accumulating at this level.
3. His core strategic view: accumulating Bitcoin after the June low and continuing to buy through the end of the midterm year — even as price moves lower — has historically been the best-performing approach.
4. The single biggest mistake he sees investors make is selling during a downturn and then never buying back, missing the eventual recovery.
5. He directly addresses the "late last year seller" who still hasn't re-entered: if you were never going to buy back, you would have been better off not selling at all.

## Predictions made
