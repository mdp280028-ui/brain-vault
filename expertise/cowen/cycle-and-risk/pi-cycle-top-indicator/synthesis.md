# Pi Cycle Top Indicator

A technical indicator using the 111-day SMA crossing the 350-day SMA times two to identify cyclical Bitcoin tops. Cowen tracks it as a secondary signal alongside his risk metric, particularly around suspected cycle tops, and discusses how its signals have shifted across cycles.
