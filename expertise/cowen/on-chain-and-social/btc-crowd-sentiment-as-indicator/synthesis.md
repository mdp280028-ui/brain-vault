## Current Thesis

Cowen uses Twitter/social polls as a crowd-sentiment gauge but explicitly rejects the reflexive contrarian reading — the majority is not automatically wrong. His framework holds that a 60/40 or even 65/35 bearish split simply means the crowd is correctly reading the bear market; the contrarian signal only fires when consensus reaches 75–80% on one side. As of early June 2026, polls show a majority calling the bear correctly, which Cowen treats as confirmation of Stage 3 rather than a reversal signal. The contrarian extreme — near-unanimous pessimism — is what he expects to coincide with the Stage 3 bottom around October 2026.
