# Evidence log: Macro Headwinds Crypto

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-06-28 (OYcGIifaO4Q): Cowen presents a presidential-cycle overlay showing BTC returns under Biden (-43.8%) and Trump's second term (-41.1%) at the 524-day mark are nearly identical and far weaker than the prior two presidential periods, and attributes this to shared macro conditions — inflation, worsening unemployment, and a strengthening dollar — rather than political party. The DXY is following the same topping/bottoming pattern across Trump's first and second terms and is now rising again, leading Cowen to forecast it returns to the 105–106 area: "My guess is that the dollar will likely head kind of back up to that 105, 106 area. And that might continue to be a macro headwind for crypto, you know, in sort of later in the year." [06:02]
