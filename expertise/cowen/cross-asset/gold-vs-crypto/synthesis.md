# Gold vs Crypto

Gold price action and the gold-vs-Bitcoin relationship. Cowen uses gold's strength as both a macro-uncertainty signal and a comparison point for Bitcoin's 'digital gold' thesis, noting gold's outperformance in late-stage bear markets.

## Current Thesis

Gold is outperforming Bitcoin on every relevant axis — political-cycle regime, midterm-year seasonality, and relative price — and Cowen continues to favor gold over Bitcoin through the Q4 2026 cycle low. As of June 2026, BTC was rejected at bare market resistance against gold, and Cowen expects Bitcoin to fall an additional ~30% against gold through the remainder of 2026 via a combination of further BTC declines and gold recovering from its near-term low. Gold is expected to find a bottom within 1–2 months (by ~August 2026) and then trend higher, reinforcing the seasonal pattern in which gold bottoms in summer and rallies in H2 while Bitcoin sells off. Structurally, the political-cycle case for gold over Bitcoin remains intact: gold thrives under the current Republican sweep while Bitcoin's median return under both party sweeps is negative, with the long-run exit signal for gold not arriving until a potential 2029–2030 Democratic president + Republican Congress regime shift.

## Key Evidence

- Gold performs best under Republican sweep (current regime); 2025 was flagged as one of gold's strongest years on record; Bitcoin's median return is negative under both party sweeps. (9a-LA7tm8Mo)
- The BTC/Gold ratio shows a well-defined high; Cowen's prior call was for Bitcoin to drop ~45% against gold by Q4 2026, now updated to ~30% additional downside from June 2026 levels, with gold expected to recover after a ~1–2 month low. (WHK1S6MrlCA, QMn7cm4nfYU)
- BTC was rejected at bare market resistance against gold in June 2026 — a technical confirmation that the ratio breakdown is intact and continuing. (QMn7cm4nfYU)
- In midterm years, gold typically bottoms around summer and rallies in H2 while Bitcoin sells off; Bitcoin would need to drop ~40% vs. gold just to return to its 2022 relative level. (8PQee-onXrU)
- Cowen explicitly favors cash and gold over Bitcoin through Q4 2026; the outlook table rates Gold ↑2 (months) against BTC ↓4 (months) / ↓3 (year). (8PQee-onXrU, QMn7cm4nfYU)
- Gold's worst regime is Democratic president + Republican Congress, which Cowen flags as a potential 2029–2030 exit signal — a concrete forward-looking inflection point. (9a-LA7tm8Mo)
