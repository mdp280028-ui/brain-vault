# ETH-BTC Ratio

The price ratio of Ethereum to Bitcoin, used by Cowen as the leading indicator for altcoin-season conditions. Sustained moves up indicate alt-favoring regime; moves down indicate BTC-favoring regime; multi-year breakdowns precede altcoin underperformance cycles.
