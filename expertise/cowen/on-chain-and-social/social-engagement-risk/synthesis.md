# Social Engagement Risk

Social-media engagement metrics (mentions, Google Trends, sentiment indices) used as a contrarian signal. High retail engagement coincides with cyclical tops; apathy coincides with cyclical bottoms in Cowen's framework.

## Current Thesis
Cowen currently reads social/retail engagement as subdued relative to the prior cycle top, which supports a "still mid-cycle, not yet capitulated" read on Bitcoin. His social interest risk metric sits around 0.25 as of July 2026, matching July 2018 rather than the hotter July 2022 (~0.5) reading, and this is corroborated by crypto YouTube view counts running roughly a third of their July 2022 pace. He frames overall market structure as more like 2019 (liquidity/business-cycle terms) while social interest specifically tracks 2018 — implying retail apathy is consistent with still being in a cyclical trough phase rather than a top.

## Key Evidence
- Social interest/risk metric currently ~0.25, matching July 2018 levels rather than July 2022's ~0.5 reading (XQN1M-sIuGs)
- Crypto YouTube channel view counts ~350K/day now vs. ~1 million/day in July 2022, used as a corroborating proxy for retail engagement (XQN1M-sIuGs)
- Cowen explicitly separates the two lenses: market structure resembles 2019, but social interest specifically resembles 2018 (XQN1M-sIuGs)

## Recent Updates
