# Apathy vs Euphoria Tops

Cowen's framework for identifying market tops and bottoms by sentiment regime — apathy and disinterest at lows, euphoria and FOMO at highs. The current sentiment state is a key read on cycle position alongside price and on-chain data.

## Current Thesis

Cowen classifies the 2025/2026 cycle top as "apathetic" rather than euphoric — the opposite emotional signature from prior blow-off tops — and treats the current, still-declining social interest and retail attention as confirmation that the market has not yet found a true bottom. He frames the post-top period as a "digestion phase" following a non-euphoric top, and argues that persistent apathy (falling YouTube views, a declining advanced-decline index, no altcoin rotation) is consistent with, rather than contradictory to, his broader 4-year cycle framework calling for further downside into a Q4 capitulation. No new sentiment-regime evidence has surfaced since; the most recent brief (zyPPtwewGaQ) is a standalone trading-psychology clip unrelated to sentiment-cycle positioning.

## Key Evidence

- Crypto YouTube channel views have fallen roughly an order of magnitude, from ~3-4M/day in 2021 to ~400K/day now, tracked as a proxy for social/retail interest (prior)
- Social/retail interest has been trending down continuously since May 2021 (prior)
- The advanced-decline index of the top 100 cryptocurrencies has been falling for years, indicating broad-based disinterest beyond just BTC price action (prior)
- This cycle featured no altcoin rotation at cycle end — the first such cycle — which Cowen reads as further evidence of an apathetic rather than euphoric top (prior)
- Cowen explicitly labels the cycle top as apathetic, not euphoric: "We're in the post drawdown digestion phase after the non-apathetic top or sorry, after the non-euphoric top. It was an apathetic top." (prior)
- On-chain resets (MVRV Z-score below zero, price undercutting realized/balance price) haven't fully occurred yet, which Cowen pairs with the apathy read as evidence more downside/capitulation is needed before a genuine bottom (prior)
