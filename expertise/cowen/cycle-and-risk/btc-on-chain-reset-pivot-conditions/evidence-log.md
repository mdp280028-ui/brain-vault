# Evidence log: Btc On Chain Reset Pivot Conditions

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-06-14 (psz9FwqWGns): Cowen explicitly defines the conditions under which he would pivot away from his October 2026 base case: a price-based capitulation — a drop of roughly $20,000 from ~$63K — that resets all on-chain indicators (realized price, MVRV-Z, volume spike, supply in profit/loss crossover). He uses March 2020 as the template, noting that crash justified an early pivot precisely because it produced a complete on-chain reset. Until that occurs, he states: [05:23] "If Bitcoin were to capitulate in June, the same way that it did in March of 2020, then it wouldn't make sense in my opinion to stay bearish until October. It just wouldn't." He also notes that even a drop to ~$54K (realized price) would likely not fully reset the MVRV-Z score, meaning partial resets do not satisfy his pivot condition.
