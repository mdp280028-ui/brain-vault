## Methodology

### Process

1. **Web search for SERP composition** on each candidate angle before pulling keywords — avoid wasted Ahrefs pulls on legal-wall or seller-wall verticals.
2. Pull 80-100 keywords per round filtered for asbestos relevance.
3. Apply gates: SV ≥ 50 (anchor), KD ≤ 8 (affiliate), CPC any value (asbestos CPCs run low).
4. Confirm SERP composition for every PASS keyword before recommending build.
5. Test for cannibalization against existing 31 shipped slugs.

### Methodology change worth keeping for future sessions

April keyword research was Ahrefs-first — pull related-terms blocks, then categorize. This session was **web-search-first** — audit the SERP composition for each candidate cluster before spending Ahrefs credits.

The change caught the legal-wall and YMYL clusters before any Ahrefs query ran. Default future research sessions to that order:

1. Web-search 5-8 SERP audits to identify candidate clusters
2. Pull 80-100 keywords across surviving clusters
3. Analyze for Tier 1/2 anchors
4. Stop at hit rate < 15% per round

30 seconds of web search per candidate angle prevents pulling 20 keywords on a cluster that turns out to be locked. This session saved roughly 200 wasted Ahrefs credits across 12 dead-on-arrival clusters (appliances, brake, marine, schools, etc.).

- **Round 1** (100 kw): Mobile homes, wood stoves, fireplaces, material scenarios → mostly 0-10 SV, dead

## Cluster analysis — what to build and why

### Why the Equipment cluster is the flagship

Combined ~3,650 SV at KD 0-2 averaging $0.45 CPC. Every single keyword in this cluster has **direct affiliate revenue**:
- Amazon Associates (HEPA vacuums $150-800, respirators $30-200, glove bags $20-50, disposal bags $30-80, warning labels $10-25, PPE suits $15-30)
- Equipment rental affiliates (Sunbelt, HD Rental, United Rentals) — $20-40 per converted rental

The April 22 monetization plan ranked this cluster #1 at $1,500-3,000/mo at maturity. Until this batch, no pages targeted it. The opportunity is to build the equipment hub before any competitor realizes the cluster is unclaimed.

### Why the Transite cluster is the second flagship

Combined ~1,840 SV at KD 0-2. Most importantly: **$7 CPC on the anchor keyword.** That CPC is bid by water utility lawyers (drinking water asbestos exposure suits) AND remediation contractors (commercial transite removal jobs).

For an editorial site staying out of legal framing, the affiliate path is:
- Encapsulation paint products (Fiberlock ABC, Foster 32-22, Rawlins Paints)
- Sealer products
- Future Phase 2 contractor referral revenue (commercial water utility lead-gen)

The SERP is genuinely clean — Inspectapedia (Daniel Friedman) + Structure Tech (Reuben Saltzman) + small contractor sites. No mesothelioma legal wall on the primary keyword (despite $7 CPC).

⚠️ Strategic flag: Stay out of "exposure," "lawsuit," "settlement" framing. The page must position as identification + repair + abatement guide. Per project rule: no mesothelioma legal CTAs.

### Why Encapsulation is the third flagship

Combined ~1,750 SV at KD 0-4. Conversion-aligned with buyer's decision tree:
1. Find suspicious material → buy test kit (existing slugs cover this)
2. Confirm asbestos → DECISION POINT
3. Path A: full removal ($8-25/sqft, abatement contractor needed)
4. Path B: encapsulation ($2-6/sqft, often DIY-allowed) — **this is where Batch 4 catches them**

Brand affiliate revenue (PerfectPrimer direct, Fiberlock via Amazon) at higher commission rates than generic Amazon affiliate. Building this hub captures decisions that currently funnel to removal contractors (who don't pay this site).

### Why Zonolite Trust is the surprise winner

Only 350 SV combined but $4 CPC and unique action-intent. Homeowners with vermiculite attic insulation can recover up to **$4,125 via the W.R. Grace ZAI Trust**. Reimbursement covers **55% of abatement costs** (max $4,125). The Trust is funded by W.R. Grace's 2014 bankruptcy resolution; minimum 20-year operation window means claims will be accepted into 2034.

The page bridges:
- Existing vermiculite-insulation-guide (Batch 1) — links to claim eligibility content
- EMSL lab referral (Zonolite-specific Barium test marker — separate from generic chrysotile/amphibole PLM)
- Cross-niche financial-action authority play

Strong content-defendable angle even at DR 0 because the topic is procedural/financial-action, not informational. Buyer is motivated.
