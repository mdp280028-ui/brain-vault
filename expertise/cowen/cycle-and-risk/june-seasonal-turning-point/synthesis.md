## Current Thesis

June is a historically consistent pivot month for Bitcoin in bear-market years: when BTC is rallying into June (as in 2014 and 2019), June tends to mark the cycle high for the countertrend rally; when BTC is falling into June (as in 2018 and 2022), June tends to mark the low. As of mid-May 2026, Bitcoin is still rallying, which Cowen reads as making a June top — not a June bottom — the more probable outcome. He expects BTC to stall somewhere between the 200-day MA and the 0.382 Fibonacci level (~$85K) by early June before rolling over into a deeper Q4 2026 decline.
