# Evidence log: Equities & SPX Correlation

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-15 (FgxAe_NAh5c): Cowen argues the four-year cycle belongs to equities first and crypto second — the S&P 500 has bottomed approximately every four years since 1958 without a halving mechanism, undermining the halving-causation narrative. He maps 2026 onto 2018: SPX already posted a Q1 correction and is on track for a second, larger drawdown in Q3/Q4, exactly as happened when the September 2018 SPX top gave way to a December crash that dragged Bitcoin to ~$3,100. Resurging inflation (PPI MoM described as "approximately 6%") keeps the Fed pinned, prolonging the macro headwind. At [10:53] he states: "I'm suggesting we've already got one under our belt in Q1. We'll likely have another big correction either in Q3 or Q4."
