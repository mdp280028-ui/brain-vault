# Sell And Never Buy Back Mistake

Auto-created topic. Seed description pending operator review. Slug-as-words: sell and never buy back mistake.

## Current Thesis

Cowen argues that selling during a Bitcoin bear-market drawdown is understandable — he himself dislikes sitting through 70–80% drops and has reduced exposure — but the fatal error is selling and never buying back. Investors who exit during downturns and fail to re-enter miss the recovery entirely, leaving them worse off than if they had never sold. His pointed formulation: if you were never going to buy back, you would have been better off not selling at all. This view is reinforced by his broader anti-cash thesis: sitting out in cash is a losing strategy because inflation continuously erodes purchasing power, making the failure to re-enter a doubly costly error.
