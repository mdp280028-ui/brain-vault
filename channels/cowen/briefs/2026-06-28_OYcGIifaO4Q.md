## Key points (the distilled video)
1. Bitcoin has existed through portions of Obama's second term, Trump's first term, Biden's term, and now Trump's second term — enough data for a meaningful comparison.
2. Obama's second term and Trump's first term produced similar, strong BTC returns; the paths differed but end results were roughly equivalent, with Bitcoin surging near the end of Trump's first term.
3. Biden's term and Trump's second term look strikingly similar to each other — and both are dramatically weaker than the prior two presidential periods.
4. At day 524 specifically: Biden's BTC return was -43.8%; Trump's second term BTC return is currently -41.1% — nearly identical despite all political differences.
5. Cowen attributes the weaker BTC performance in the Biden/Trump-2 era to macro shifts: inflation became a concern, unemployment trends worsened, and these headwinds suppressed riskier assets — not political party affiliation.
6. Under Biden at this same point in the cycle, Bitcoin then stalled through summer, had a brief counter-trend rally in late July/early August, sold off, made one final low, and then entered the next bull market.
7. The DXY (dollar index) is showing the same pattern across Trump's first and second terms: topped in January 2017 / January 2025, bottomed in January–February 2018 / February 2026, then began grinding higher — Cowen has been calling for this dollar rise all year.
8. Cowen expects the dollar to continue heading back toward the 105–106 area, which he flags as a continued macro headwind for crypto later in 2026.
9. Core takeaway: Bitcoin is not a political asset — its performance tracks macro conditions and cycle dynamics far more than which party holds the presidency.
10. If the current cycle continues mirroring Biden's term, Bitcoin would be expected to bottom later in 2026 and then begin the next bull market into 2027.

## Predictions made
- Dollar (DXY) likely heads back up to the 105–106 area, acting as a macro headwind for crypto later in 2026.
- If the Biden-cycle analog holds, BTC bottoms later in 2026 and begins the next bull market into 2027.
