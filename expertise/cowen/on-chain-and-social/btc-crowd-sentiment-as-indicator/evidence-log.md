# Evidence log: Btc Crowd Sentiment As Indicator

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-06-07 (gkDqbvMnMHI): Cowen uses Twitter/social polls as a crowd-sentiment contrarian indicator, but refines the rule: a 60/40 split means the majority can simply be right. The reversal signal only activates at 75–80% consensus. Current readings of roughly 60–65% bearish tell him the crowd has correctly identified the bear market, not that it is about to end. He ties the eventual sentiment extreme to his Stage 3 thesis: "once everyone believes in the bare market, that's usually when the bare market ends" [13:03].
