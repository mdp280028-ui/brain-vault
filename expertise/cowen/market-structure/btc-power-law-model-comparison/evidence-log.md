# Evidence log: Btc Power Law Model Comparison

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-30 (uFn3KUE-VTI): Cowen positions his new asymmetric tail curvature quantile model within a direct lineage of prior Bitcoin price frameworks — rainbow chart, power law, regression rainbow, Real Plan C — each correcting a predecessor's specific failure. He is explicit that the power law model retains full parity at the lower tail: "This at the lows at the lows is no better than the original power law model. No better." [11:15]. The new model's only claimed edge is the upper tail, where it captures the converging, diminishing curvature of cycle peaks that stock-to-flow overshoots and single-curve models cannot represent. The long-run price table extending to 2050 provides quantitative constraints that implicitly rebut more aggressive power-law-derived $1M timelines.
