# Evidence log: Post-Halving Behavior

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-11 (Li3m6eEWECA): Cowen presents a structural comparison between the current BTC cycle and 2018, noting that both followed a February low, an April higher low, and a May rally toward the 200-day moving average — replicated at 10x price levels ($6K/$6,400 then vs. ~$60K/~$65K now). He describes it as "actually really similar to how it played out in 2018" [00:35] but explicitly refuses to conclude whether the cycle will resolve like 2018 (further decline) or 2019 (recovery). The fractal pattern spanning multiple halving cycles supports his broader tendency to treat halving-correlated price action as coincident market structure rather than halving-driven causation.
