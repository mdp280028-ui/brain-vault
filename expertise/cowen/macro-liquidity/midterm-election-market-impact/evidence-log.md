# Evidence log: Midterm Election Market Impact

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-13 (9a-LA7tm8Mo): Using S&P 500 data from 1953 and Bitcoin data from 2013, Cowen identifies midterm years as the worst in the presidential cycle for both assets — SPX running "fine" at +8% YTD but historically weak, while BTC faces a historical midterm average of −65%. He flags the current Republican sweep as likely to transition post-2026 into the most bearish equity regime (Republican president + Democratic Congress, avg 4.9%), where most historical recessions have occurred. [23:00] "You can see the midterm year on average is -65% on average. What has Bitcoin done so far this year? Well, it's down. About 7%."
