## TL;DR
Cowen argues the S&P 500's playbook from prior midterm years (shallow June dip, July rally, larger 10-20% drop in August/September) is repeating in 2026, and that a Bitcoin market cycle bottom typically coincides with that back-half-of-year stock market correction.

## Key points (the distilled video)
1. Cowen previously called for a shallow June correction followed by a July rally in the S&P — he says this played out as expected, since June rarely marks a major top for stocks.
2. July has been a green month for the S&P 500 every year since 2015 on average monthly returns; August and September are the two red months on average, a pattern he says is even more pronounced in midterm years.
3. His framework: a distribution phase with a short shallow drop, a rally back to new highs/sideways action, then a larger drop — he says the S&P already did the first drop and is now in the rally/sideways phase, setting up for a bigger drop around August-September.
4. In the last three midterm years, the S&P topped and dropped meaningfully: 2022 topped mid-August and fell ~19%; 2018 topped mid-September and fell ~20%; 2014 saw a correction starting in September of about 10%.
5. All three of those midterm years (2022, 2018, 2014) also had a small correction in June before the larger correction later in the year, matching the current 2026 pattern.
6. Comparing 2026's year-to-date S&P ROI to 2018, 2014, and 2022 individually, and to an average of all three midterm years, shows the same shape: early correction, summer rally, then a bigger correction in the back half of the year that can leave the market weak for the rest of the year.
7. Cowen ties this to Bitcoin: he says Bitcoin normally finds its market cycle bottom during the stock market correction that occurs in the back half of a midterm year, so weakness in BTC alongside an SPX correction in August/September could mark the four-year cycle bottom aligning for both assets.
8. He speculates (without strong conviction) that the trigger for the anticipated correction could come from the semiconductor industry, noting semis have gotten "pretty elevated" and a correction there could tie into the broader seasonal SPX correction.

## Predictions made
- S&P 500 seasonal correction of roughly 10-20% expected to start in the August-September 2026 time frame, based on the pattern in the last three midterm years (2022, 2018, 2014).
- Bitcoin's market cycle bottom is expected to coincide with that same back-half-of-2026 stock market correction, potentially aligning the four-year BTC cycle low with an S&P low.
