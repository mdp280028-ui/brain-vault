# Evidence log: Fibonacci 382 Target 85k

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-14 (zKHgKnv51r0): Cowen uses the Fibonacci retracement tool measured from the cycle high to the most recent low and finds the 0.382 level sits near $85K in the current cycle — a level that has been the consistent stall point for countertrend rallies across the 2014, 2018, and 2022 bear markets. This level also converges with a descending trendline channel by early June, making it dual-confluence resistance. He allows that a deeper extension toward the 0.5 level (the 90s) is possible if the 2019 analog plays out fully, but treats $85K as the base-case ceiling. Direct quote: "If it does get through, like 2014, that 0.382 might come into play, which is right around 85." [08:50]
