# Evidence log: Bull Market Support Band

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-25 (dy3E7Jzte88): In this video Cowen observes that Bitcoin, trading around $77K, has been rejected at the 20-week SMA / 21-week EMA band — which he now labels the "bear market resistance band" — mirroring rejections seen in every prior midterm bear year. Critically, he extends the indicator to the BTC/SPX ratio, noting it too was rejected at the same level: "If you look at midterm years, Bitcoin's valuation against the S&P frequently gets rejected at that 20we SMA, 21week EMA, and that's exactly where it just got rejected at." [05:44] This dual rejection (spot BTC and the ratio) underpins his call for BTC to underperform SPX for several more months and for a sweep of the ~$60K April low within the next couple of months.
