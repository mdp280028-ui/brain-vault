# Evidence log: Spacex Ipo Valuation And Lockup

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-06-18 (xutthOFM_RA): The SpaceX IPO opened at ~$130 and ran to a high near $220 (~50% gain), but the first major insider unlock — releasing over 20% of the float — hits August 11th, and all three hosts expect selling pressure to follow. Guy frames the current 130x revenue valuation as "insane" and draws a historical parallel to Tesla's IPO, noting that ~90% of IPOs eventually trade below their IPO-day price and that SpaceX launched at a valuation "many orders of magnitude higher" than Tesla's was at its IPO. Despite the stretched valuation, Guy signals genuine long-term interest once the post-unlock dip materializes, possibly as early as August.
