# BTC Cycle Theory

Cowen's framework that Bitcoin and broader risk assets follow an approximately four-year cycle of bull-top, bear-bottom, and accumulation phases. He frames the cycle as macro-driven rather than halving-driven, citing parallel equity-market cycle precedents going back to the 1950s.

## Current Thesis

BTC is in Stage 3 of the bear market (June–October 2026), with the cycle bottom pinned at approximately day 1,424–1,432 from the cycle low — roughly late Q3/early Q4 2026 — based on Cowen's primary indicator (ROI from cycle low), which nailed the prior two tops within one week and is given "50% of attention" for timing the bottom. The four-year cycle rule — buy at end of midterm years, sell in Q4 of the post-halving year — remains undefeated across three cycles, and Cowen is explicitly more bearish near-term (months conviction ↓3), expecting further downside before a counter-trend rally in mid-to-late July, then the final drop into Q4. Multiple on-chain conditions remain unsatisfied (MVRV Z-Score at 0.251, not yet below zero; BTC not yet below the realized price ~$53K; no volume capitulation spike), and Cowen now names the balance price (~$38K) as the clearest price-based capitulation signal — if hit, it would fully reset all on-chain metrics and make the bottom call straightforward, though he does not require it to happen.

## Key Evidence

- BTC topped on cycle day 1,162 — within one week of the prior two cycles' tops (days 1,059 and 1,168); the ROI-from-low indicator (Cowen's single highest-weight timer, "50% of attention") places the bottom at approximately day 1,424–1,432, which is ~100–110 days from the June 25 broadcast, pointing to late Q3/early Q4 2026. (7byCfkDQxq8, QMn7cm4nfYU, psz9FwqWGns, g4FiHz14W14)
- Every midterm-year bear market has lasted approximately 50–60 weeks: Dec 2017→Dec 2018 (~53 wks), Nov 2021→Nov 2022 (~54 wks), 2013–14 (~59 wks); prior cycles also bottomed at days 364–406 from the peak, while the current cycle is only at day 262 from peak, confirming more time is needed. (psz9FwqWGns, hXlR5xu0HK4, g4FiHz14W14)
- The MVRV Z-Score is currently 0.251, approaching zero; every prior cycle has seen it cross below zero before the bull market began, and that reset has not yet occurred — Cowen expects it may cross zero later in 2026. (hXlR5xu0HK4, g4FiHz14W14)
- In every prior bear market, Bitcoin dipped below the realized price (~$53K currently) before the cycle bottom was confirmed; BTC has not yet done so, but Cowen views it as likely ("I doubt it" that this time is different), consistent with 2018 (not until November) and 2014 (not until September/October). (R9vEpIpyj2s, psz9FwqWGns, g4FiHz14W14)
- Every historical Bitcoin bear market has ended with a massive weekly volume capitulation spike (seen in 2018 and 2022 after extended low-volume periods); that spike has not yet appeared in the current cycle. The balance price (~$38K) — where Bitcoin historically spends only a few days — is the clearest price-based all-clear signal if triggered. (psz9FwqWGns, g4FiHz14W14)
- Cowen's distilled rule — buy BTC at the end of midterm years, sell at the end of post-halving years — is confirmed undefeated across three cycles; "the four-year cycle didn't fail at the top. Maybe it won't fail at the bottom." (slTIiS-Y65k, Tj7mvTHDe1U, dIa8HUYDNEY, psz9FwqWGns, vTcABO_0Exg, hXlR5xu0HK4, 5nIhiijBQt4, g4FiHz14W14)
