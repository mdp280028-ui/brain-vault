# Bitcoin Dominance

BTC's market-cap share of the total crypto market. Cowen tracks dominance as the primary lens for altcoin-season timing — falling dominance signals capital rotation into alts, rising dominance signals BTC-favoring regime.

## Current Thesis

BTC dominance continues to reflect a BTC-over-alts regime, reinforced by declining social/retail interest, a hawkish macro backdrop, and 2018/2019 historical analogs pointing to altcoin underperformance while BTC chops sideways to lower. This brief adds an explicit new data point: this is the first cycle in Cowen's framework where cycle end brought no altcoin rotation at all, with 2025/2026 mapped directly onto 2019/2020 (matching three rate cuts, QT ending ~two months after BTC's cycle top in both cases) as the closest analog. With on-chain indicators (MVRV Z-score, realized/balance price) not yet reset to prior-cycle bottom levels and BTC still squeezed between the 200-week MA and bear market resistance band, Cowen sees no near-term catalyst for capital to rotate into alts — the BTC-favoring regime holds through an expected Q4 time-based capitulation and into a possible bottom by his November ITC conference.

## Key Evidence

- BTC dominance excluding stablecoins still making higher highs; as social interest trends down, dominance trends up — a relationship Cowen says has held since 2021 (gAf6tPzkcgg)
- Social risk composite (Google Trends, Coinbase app ranking, YouTube subscribers/views, Wikipedia page views, Twitter followers) in sustained decline; crypto YouTube channels now losing ~400–500 subscribers/week vs. gaining 40,000–60,000/week at the 2021 peak (gAf6tPzkcgg)
- 2018 analog: altcoins were "annihilated" in July 2018 while BTC traded sideways; declining social interest makes a repeat plausible in 2026 (d71wYhwKpFY)
- 2019/2020 analog now framed as this cycle's closest precedent and explicitly the first cycle-end with zero altcoin rotation: matching three rate cuts and QT ending ~two months after BTC's cycle top in both 2019 and 2025/2026 (sigSZCnSa6M)
- Crypto YouTube views down roughly an order of magnitude, from ~3-4M/day in 2021 to ~400K/day now, alongside a multi-year decline in the advanced-decline index of the top 100 cryptocurrencies — broad apathy consistent with no rotation catalyst (sigSZCnSa6M)
- Total 2 (minus USDT) / BTC ratio in relentless decline as rate-cut expectations evaporate; zero cuts priced through 2027, removing the macro catalyst alts require (Rf-gAQJk5-0)
