# Evidence log: DXY Dollar Strength

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-13 (9a-LA7tm8Mo): Cowen presents DXY performance broken down by political regime (data since 1980), finding that the dollar declines the most under a Republican sweep — the current configuration — and performs best under a Democratic president paired with a Republican Congress. He also notes that midterm years have historically been weak for the dollar on average, but that strong dollar readings in the 2018 and 2022 midterms have created recency bias distorting that impression. No direct DXY quote is provided in the brief, but the finding is framed as a forward-looking regime signal relevant to risk-asset positioning.
