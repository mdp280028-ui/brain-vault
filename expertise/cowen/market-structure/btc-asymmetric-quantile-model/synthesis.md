## Current Thesis

Cowen has introduced a new asymmetric tail curvature quantile model for Bitcoin that fits the lower and upper price tails with mathematically distinct curvature, superseding his earlier regression rainbow and non-rigorous quantile attempts. The model's central claim is that the upper tail is where prior frameworks (stock-to-flow, simple power law) fail — successive cycle peaks converge and diminish, meaning Bitcoin tops out well short of prior euphoria levels. With BTC currently at the 9.4th historical percentile, the model frames current price as deep value territory relative to history, while the upper tail analysis explains both the absence of a durable alt season and the structural ceiling on cycle peaks.
