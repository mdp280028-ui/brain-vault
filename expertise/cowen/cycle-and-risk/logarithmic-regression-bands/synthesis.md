# Logarithmic Regression Bands

Log-regression bands fitted to Bitcoin's all-time price history that Cowen uses to define long-term support, midline, and resistance trajectories. He treats deviation from the bands as a long-horizon valuation signal and a complement to the risk metric.

## Current Thesis

Cowen uses logarithmic regression bands to frame ETH's long-term valuation across three levels: a lower support band, a fair-value midline, and an upper bear market resistance band. ETH has now been rejected at the resistance band and Cowen expects it to fall to the lower band — a level that maps exactly to the April 2025 lows — mirroring the 2019 cycle analog in which ETH did the same before staging a substantial rally. The fair-value midline only crossed $2,000 in 2026, validating his long-standing call that ETH would "go home" before any new all-time high. A durable breach below the lower band would signal recession; a touch without recession is his trigger for a counter-trend rally.

## Key Evidence

- ETH rejected at the bear market resistance band (upper band) in May 2026, confirming the band as meaningful overhead resistance in the current cycle (mwrgOz852wI)
- The lower regression band aligns precisely with the April 2025 lows, providing a concrete near-term price target for the anticipated decline (mwrgOz852wI)
- In 2021, Cowen predicted the regression model's fair value for ETH would not cross $2,000 until 2026; ETH is now trading near that midline level, validating the model's long-range accuracy (mwrgOz852wI)
- The 2019 cycle analog: ETH previously fell to the lower regression band before staging a ~240% rally, supporting the lower band as a meaningful cyclical floor (mwrgOz852wI)
- Brief dips below the lower band (e.g., March 2020) have historically coincided with recession-level macro stress, making a durable break below the band a recession signal (mwrgOz852wI)
- A touch of the lower band without a recession materializing is Cowen's explicit trigger for expecting a "sizable counter-trend move" upward (mwrgOz852wI)
