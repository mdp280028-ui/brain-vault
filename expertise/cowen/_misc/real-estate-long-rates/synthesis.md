## Current Thesis

Cowen raises the structural possibility that the decades-long era of falling long-term rates has ended, with 30-year Treasury yields approaching or exceeding their October 2023 highs. If this secular shift is real, it is most damaging to zombie companies and high-risk assets, and implies persistent headwinds for real-estate-sensitive sectors that benefited from cheap long-term financing. He treats this not as a confirmed call but as a live risk scenario that investors should price in.
