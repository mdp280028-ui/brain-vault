# Gold vs Crypto

Gold price action and the gold-vs-Bitcoin relationship. Cowen uses gold's strength as both a macro-uncertainty signal and a comparison point for Bitcoin's 'digital gold' thesis, noting gold's outperformance in late-stage bear markets.

## Current Thesis

Gold is outperforming Bitcoin on every relevant axis — political-cycle regime, midterm-year seasonality, and relative price — and Cowen explicitly holds gold (alongside cash) over Bitcoin through the Q4 2026 cycle low. Structurally, gold thrives under the current Republican sweep while Bitcoin posts negative median returns under both party sweeps, undermining the "digital gold" thesis at the political-cycle level. In midterm years specifically, gold typically bottoms around summer and rallies through H2 while Bitcoin sells off; Bitcoin would need to fall ~40–45% against gold just to revisit its 2022 relative level, with Cowen's BTC target of ~$40K by Q4 2026 implying the ratio has far further to fall. The long-run exit signal for gold remains a potential 2029–2030 shift to Democratic president + Republican Congress, historically the worst regime for the metal.

## Key Evidence

- Gold performs best under Republican sweep (current regime); 2025 was flagged as one of gold's strongest years on record. (9a-LA7tm8Mo)
- Bitcoin's median return under both Democratic and Republican sweeps is negative (−2% avg / −6% median), while gold is positive under those same regimes — a structural divergence in political-cycle sensitivity. (9a-LA7tm8Mo)
- The BTC/Gold ratio shows a well-defined high and a potentially well-defined low; Cowen states Bitcoin is likely to drop ~45% against gold by Q4 2026 as Bitcoin trends to ~$40K and gold continues higher. (WHK1S6MrlCA)
- In midterm years, gold typically bottoms around summer and rallies in H2 while Bitcoin sells off; Bitcoin would need to drop ~40% vs. gold just to return to its 2022 relative level — "crazy" sounding, but nearly everything else in crypto has already done that. (8PQee-onXrU)
- Cowen explicitly favors cash and gold over Bitcoin through Q4 2026; the outlook table rates Gold ↑3 (year) / ↑2 (months) against BTC ↓3 (year) / ↓2 (months) / ↓2 (weeks). (8PQee-onXrU)
- Gold's worst regime is Democratic president + Republican Congress, which Cowen flags as a potential 2029–2030 exit signal — a concrete forward-looking inflection point. (9a-LA7tm8Mo)
