# Evidence log: Altcoin Bleed Thesis

Append-only. Newest entries at bottom.

- 2026-05-19 (M5d5fbiSfhc): Cowen uses the resolution of his altcoin bleed thesis as a methodological anchor for his current BTC bear case — he argues that framing alt/BTC targets as "reaching range lows" was always the optimistic interpretation, because the structurally correct read was a downtrend with lower highs and lower lows, which is exactly what played out. He further notes that altcoin season was mechanically cut short in Q4 2025 because Bitcoin entered a bear market before speculative euphoria could accumulate, validating the causal mechanism behind the bleed thesis. He explicitly maps this same "optimistic = range lows, realistic = structural downtrend" logic onto BTC's current position, extending the thesis from an alt/BTC observation into a general analytical framework.
